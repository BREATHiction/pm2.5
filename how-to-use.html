How to Use

1. Choose what you want to measure using the two calculators. “Short-term” or “Long-term”.

2. Once you have chosen, Select your city and click “Check Air-Quality” button to get PM2.5 concentration data.

3. Count how many breaths you can make in a minute, you may utilize the timer provided.

4. Enter the other details, Age, Body Weight, and Length of Exposure (Only if you chose Short-Term) if not, only the other details Age and Body Weight will be needed.

5. Click “Calculate” to see your results!!
